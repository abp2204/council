# Project Brief: LeetCode for Lawyers

## Vision
A gamified legal practice platform where lawyers sharpen their argumentation skills by simulating real courtroom battles against AI opponents built from actual case transcripts.

## Core Concept
- Ingest real courtroom transcripts to build detailed profiles of lawyers, judges, and opposing counsel
- Let users argue cases against an AI opponent that mimics real historical legal strategy
- Score users on legal soundness, strategic effectiveness, and creativity
- Allow users to deviate from the historical outcome and see the consequences play out — rewarding creative legal thinking, not just mimicry

## What Makes It Different
Most legal education tools are rigid and passive. This platform is interactive and dynamic — you can follow the winning lawyer's playbook, or go your own way and learn why it worked or didn't. The feedback loop is the product.

## Target Users
- Individual lawyers wanting to sharpen skills
- Law students / bar prep
- Law firms onboarding associates
- Law schools as a teaching tool

## Business Model
- Individual subscriptions
- Licensing to law schools and bar prep companies
- Enterprise deals with law firms

## MVP Scope
- Start with a handful of famous cases across a few different practice areas
- Analyze transcripts for observable patterns: argument structure, successful objections, evidence usage
- Build the simulation and scoring layer on top of that foundation

## North Star
If a lawyer comes off a session feeling like they just went 10 rounds with Thurgood Marshall — and learned something — the product works.
